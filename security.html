Security at DRecon

Security is built into everything we do. Here is how we protect your commission data.

devices Your Data Never Leaves Your Browser

The most important security feature of DRecon is also the simplest: your commission data is processed entirely within your browser. When you upload a carrier CSV statement, the file is read and analyzed locally on your device. No raw commission data is ever transmitted to or stored on our servers.

lock Your CSV files are never uploaded to any server.

This means:

  • Your carrier statements stay on your computer
  • No policyholder data ever touches our infrastructure
  • Even if our servers were compromised, your commission data would not be at risk

cloud_done Secure Infrastructure

DRecon is hosted on Vercel, an enterprise-grade hosting platform trusted by thousands of companies worldwide. This provides:

https
Automatic HTTPS encryption

All connections are encrypted with TLS. Plain-text HTTP is never used.

DDoS protection

Enterprise-level distributed denial-of-service mitigation on every request.

verified
99.99% uptime SLA

Vercel's global edge network ensures DRecon is always available when you need it.

system_update
Automatic security patches

Infrastructure is patched and updated automatically — no manual maintenance required.

person_outline Registration Data

When you register for a free audit, we collect only:

  • Your name
  • Agency name
  • Work email
  • Phone number (optional)

This information is transmitted securely via encrypted HTTPS connections and stored with Formspree, which maintains SOC 2 compliance.

We never collect:

  • Policyholder names or personal information
  • Social Security Numbers
  • Credit card or banking information
  • Policy details beyond what you choose to upload

table_chart What We Store vs What We Process

Data Status
Name, agency, email, phone STORED
Carrier CSV files PROCESSED — NEVER STORED
Commission amounts & policy numbers PROCESSED — NEVER STORED
Reconciliation results PROCESSED — NEVER STORED

Everything processed in your browser session disappears when you close the tab.

picture_as_pdf PDF Reports

When you export a PDF dispute report, it is generated entirely within your browser using client-side JavaScript. The PDF is never uploaded to or processed by our servers. The file downloads directly to your device.

manage_accounts Your Responsibilities

To keep your account secure:

  • Do not share your registered email with others
  • Use DRecon on trusted devices only
  • Contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorized use of your account

mail Contact Our Security Team

If you discover a security vulnerability or have concerns about data security, please contact us immediately. We take all security reports seriously and will respond within 24 hours.
