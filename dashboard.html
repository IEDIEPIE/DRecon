analytics How Net Variance Is Calculated

Net Variance represents the net difference between received and expected commission: Received − Expected.

To verify this, we perform a complete breakdown of all reconciliation outcomes:

  • Overpaid: Commission paid above the expected amount.
  • Underpaid: Commission owed but not fully paid.
  • Clawbacks: Commission recouped by carrier.

This math board confirms that individual underpayments, overpayments, and clawbacks combine to equal the overall Net Variance.
